About Me

Hello, I'm Michele, a Technical Sound Designer and Audio Programmer with a passion for EDM and IDM. I craft immersive audio experiences for film and games.

I specialize in crafting immersive sonic experiences for film, games, and interactive applications. I excel in creating spatialized soundscapes using Dolby Renderer in Nuendo and tools like Izotope Rx for post-production. Integrating Unreal Engine 5 with Visual Studio (or Wwise), I bring these experiences to life in video games. Proficient in C++, C#, Python, Lua, Rust, SQL, and frameworks like JUCE, I develop advanced audio software and tools.
